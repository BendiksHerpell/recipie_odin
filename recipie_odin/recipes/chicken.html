Chicken breast

Healthy and full of protein

Ingredients

  • 500g of chicken breast
  • Spices of choice
  • olive oil

Step by step

  1. slice your chicken breast in the middle to get two thinner pieces
  2. cut the thin pieces in cubes around the same size
  3. heat up some olive oil on medium heat
  4. Add the chicken and let it cook for 3 min
  5. Add the spices
  6. When the chicken is golden and brown flip it
  7. Let it cook until the other side is brown and golden
  8. Serve the chicken together with rice or a sauce of your choice
