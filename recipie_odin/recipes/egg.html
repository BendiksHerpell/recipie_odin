Fried Egg

Super fast and tasty breakfast

Ingredients

  • one or two eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Pepper, salt and other spices of choice
  • fresh greens

How to fry the egg

  1. Heat up a bit of olive oil on medium heat
  2. carefully crack the eggs so that the yolk does not break
  3. Let it fry for about 2-3 min
  4. While frying add your seasoning
  5. If the egg is crisp and brown flip it on the other side
  6. Let it sit for about a minute
  7. Serve with toast or bacon
