Lasagna

Super tasty lasaga for the whole family

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

How to make lasagna step by step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
